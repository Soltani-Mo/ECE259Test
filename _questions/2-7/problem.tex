\problem{\textit{Refraction of electrostatic field lines.} Fig.~Q2.4 shows lines of an electrostatic field near a dielectric-dielectric boundary that is free of charge ($\rho_x = 0$). Which of the following is a possible combination of the two media?
\begin{enumerate}[label=(\Alph*)]\setlength{\parskip}{1pt}
\item Medium 1 is PVC ($\varepsilon_{r1} = 2.7$) and medium 2 is mica ($\varepsilon_{r2} = 5.4$).
\item Medium 1 is mica ($\varepsilon_{r2} = 5.4$) and medium 2 is PVC ($\varepsilon_{r1} = 2.7$).
\item {Medium 1 is water ($\varepsilon_{r1} = 81$) and medium 2 is air ($\varepsilon_{r2} = 1$).}
\item Medium 1 is air ($\varepsilon_{r1} = 1$) and medium 2 is water ($\varepsilon_{r2} = 81$).
\item Both combinations (A) and (B) above.
\item Both combinations (C) and (D) above.
\end{enumerate}
