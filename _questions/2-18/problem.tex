\problem{\textit{Capacitor consisting of seven parallel plates}. The capacitor shown in Fig.~Q2.8 consist of seven parallel square metallic plates of edge length $a$ and separations between all adjacent $d$ ($d \ll a$). The medium is air. With $C$ designating the capacitance of an air-filled parallel-plate capacitor of area $a^2$ and the plate separation $d$, the capacitance of the capacitor in Fig.~Q2.8 equals
\begin{enumerate}[label=(\Alph*)]\setlength{\parskip}{1pt}
\item $C/6$.
\item $C/3$.
\item $C$.
\item $3C$.
\item {$6C$.}
\end{enumerate}
