\problem{\textit{Force on a point charge above a conducting plane.} A point charge $Q$ is situated in air at a height $h$ above a grounded conducting plane. Relative to the plane, the electric force on this charge is
\begin{enumerate}[label=(\Alph*)]\setlength{\parskip}{1pt}
\item {always attractive (plane attracts the charge).}
\item always repulsive (plane repels the charge).
\item attractive for $Q$ positive and repulsive for $Q$ negative.
\item attractive for $Q$ negative and repulsive for $Q$ positive.
\item always zero.
\end{enumerate}}
